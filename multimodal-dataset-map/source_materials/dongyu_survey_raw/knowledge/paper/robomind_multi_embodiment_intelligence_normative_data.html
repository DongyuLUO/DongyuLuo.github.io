RoboMIND: Benchmark on Multi-embodiment Intelligence Normative Data for Robot Manipulation

Wu, Hou, Liu, Che, Ju, Yang, Li, Zhao, Xu, et al. · 2025 · RSS 2025 / arXiv · arXiv:2412.13877 · PDF

Dongyu supplement. Added as a candidate missing/adjacent dataset paper after searching primary arXiv sources. This is a draft summary for triage, not a full paper read.

One-liner. A large multi-embodiment teleoperation dataset with language task descriptions, proprioception, multi-view observations, and failure demonstrations with causes.

Setup

  • Datasets / benchmarks: Introduces RoboMIND: 107k demonstration trajectories, 479 tasks, 96 object classes, four robot embodiments, plus 5k real-world failure demonstrations with detailed causes.
  • Hardware / simulator: Franka Emika Panda, UR5e, AgileX dual-arm robot, and humanoid robot with dual dexterous hands. Includes multi-view observations and proprioceptive robot state.

Method

A standardized collection platform and protocol for multi-embodiment robot manipulation data; includes Isaac Sim digital twins for additional data/evaluation.

Why it matters for the map

The failure demonstrations with causes are valuable for the project's interest in correction, monitoring, and dynamic task failure.

Limitations / open questions

Large-scale visual/proprioceptive/language corpus, but tactile/audio/thermal are not the core modalities.

Source note

arXiv lines 35-39 report dataset scale, modalities, embodiments, failures, digital twin, and project link.

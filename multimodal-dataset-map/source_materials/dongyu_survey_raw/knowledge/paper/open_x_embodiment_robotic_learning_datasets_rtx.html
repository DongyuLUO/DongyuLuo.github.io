Open X-Embodiment: Robotic Learning Datasets and RT-X Models

Open X-Embodiment Collaboration, O'Neill, Rehman, Gupta, et al. · 2024 · ICRA 2024 / arXiv · arXiv:2310.08864 · PDF

Dongyu supplement. Added as a candidate missing/adjacent dataset paper after searching primary arXiv sources. This is a draft summary for triage, not a full paper read.

One-liner. A major cross-institution robot learning dataset aggregation and RT-X model release over many robots, tasks, and embodiments.

Setup

  • Datasets / benchmarks: Assembles robotic learning datasets in a standardized format from 22 robots and 21 institutions, demonstrating 527 skills and 160,266 tasks according to the arXiv abstract/search result.
  • Hardware / simulator: 22 different robot embodiments across many institutions.

Method

Dataset aggregation plus high-capacity RT-X model training for cross-robot transfer.

Why it matters for the map

Important background for generalist VLA datasets and a reference point for what current open robot-data scale looks like.

Limitations / open questions

Mostly a vision/action/language foundation-model dataset aggregation; less targeted at non-visual state-change concept grounding.

Source note

arXiv page gives title/version; arXiv abstract/search snippet reports the 22-robot, 21-institution, 527-skill scale.
